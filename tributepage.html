ABOUT MY ROLE MODEL

She is a graduate of the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta where she studied Agricultural Administration. She is currently running a master's programme in the same University studying the same course. She is a lover of God, an Educator and a proud mother of one. One might think why will a graduate of Agricultural Administration decided to be an educator, the answer is not far fetched, this is because she finds pleasure in loves to just because she likes to encourage and motivate people especially teenagers and youths
